I created Ideation Networks because I wanted to bring open source solutions to people who needed them. I wanted to save people from proprietary vendor lock in and promote interoperability and openness.

I choose open source because I believe in Freedom. Open source gives us adaptability, interoperability, and options. It helps us prepare for the future.

Proprietary systems and data formats create lock-in where vendors build their business models around selling you licenses to access your own systems and data.

We do things differently. We build our business models on providing value.

We like to work on projects that do good. Our clients like to work with us because of the insight we offer, our methodical reasoning, and ability to generate novel solutions.
